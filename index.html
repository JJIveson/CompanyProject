Ethos

Inspired by Nature

Sabó evolved from our commitment to become part of the shift towards a plastic-free, more sustainable lifestyle.

Ingredients

Packaging

Central to our values is reducing waste to leave as little trace as possible. All our packaging comes from either recycled materials, is recyclable, biodegradable, compostable, or a combination of all these.
